EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

1. Commencement
The employment shall commence on 1st July 2025.

2. Duties
2.1 The Employee shall perform such duties as assigned by the Employer.
2.2 The Employer may, at its sole discretion, modify the duties and responsibilities of the Employee without providing any reasons.

3. Compensation
3.1 The Employee shall be paid a monthly salary as agreed.
3.2 The Employer reserves the right to revise the salary structure at any time without prior notice.

4. Termination
4.1 This Agreement may be terminated by either party by giving thirty (30) days prior written notice.
4.2 The Employer may terminate the employment immediately and without notice for any reason.

5. Governing Law
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
