EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 1st January 2025, between TechCorp Pvt. Ltd. ("Employer") and Rahul Sharma ("Employee").

1. Commencement
This agreement shall commence on 1st January 2025 and shall continue until terminated in accordance with the provisions herein.

2. Definitions
For the purpose of this agreement, the term "Employee" refers to the individual hired by the Employer under the terms and conditions set forth herein. The term "Employer" refers to TechCorp Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the laws of India.

3. Compensation
The employee shall receive a monthly compensation of INR 75,000 and benefits as agreed in writing, including health insurance and provident fund contributions as per company policy.

4. Termination Without Notice
The company reserves the right to terminate employment at any time without notice and without providing any reason, at the sole discretion of the management.

5. Termination With Notice
Either party may terminate this agreement with thirty (30) days prior written notice delivered in writing to the other party.

6. Governing Law
This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising under this agreement shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Bangalore.

7. Modification of Terms
The employer may modify terms and conditions of employment at its sole discretion without prior consultation with the employee or any written agreement.

8. Conduct and Discipline
Failure to comply with company policies may result in immediate suspension of the employee without pay and without any prior written notice or hearing.

9. Severance
In case of termination without cause, severance compensation equivalent to two months salary shall be provided to the employee along with all pending dues.

10. Confidentiality
The employee shall maintain strict confidentiality regarding all business information, trade secrets, and client data both during and after the period of employment.

10.1 The employee shall not disclose any confidential information to any third party without prior written consent of the employer.

10.2 Breach of confidentiality may result in immediate termination and legal action at the sole discretion of the employer.

11. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to annual paid leave of 18 days per year according to company policy, in addition to public holidays as declared by the Employer.

12. Probation
The employee shall be on probation for a period of six months from the date of commencement, during which the employer reserves the right to dismiss the employee immediately without notice or reason.

13. Intellectual Property
All work produced by the employee during employment shall be the exclusive property of the employer. The employer reserves the right to use, modify, or commercialize such work at any time without compensation to the employee.

14. Dispute Resolution
Any dispute arising from this agreement shall first be attempted to be resolved through mutual discussion. If unresolved within thirty (30) days, either party may initiate arbitration proceedings with prior written notice.

15. Prior Written Notice
Any changes to the salary structure shall be communicated to the employee with thirty (30) days prior written notice and shall require written acknowledgment by both parties.

16. Non-Compete
The employee agrees not to work for any competing organization for a period of one year after leaving employment, failing which the employer reserves the right to seek legal remedy without any notice.

17. Interpretation
This agreement shall be interpreted in accordance with standard legal principles applicable in India. Headings are for convenience only and shall not affect interpretation.

18. Medical Benefits
The employee shall be entitled to medical insurance coverage of INR 3,00,000 per annum for self and family, with compensation for any out-of-pocket medical expenses as agreed in writing.

19. Performance Review
Performance reviews shall be conducted every six months by either party. Compensation revisions shall be made by either party based on documented performance outcomes communicated in prior written notice.

20. Termination for Misconduct
The employer reserves the right to dismiss employees immediately for misconduct without any inquiry, warning, or opportunity to explain, at the sole discretion of the management.
